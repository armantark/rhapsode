"Done reciting" flips to the adjustment screen: every line's text appears beside its number, your live flags pre-marked, tap to correct, then confirm.

Reverse · cue layer

meaning_recall — translation as the cue

The line's existing translation annotation is the prompt; recite (aloud) the original, then reveal and self-grade. Trains the meaning→form mapping that pure serial rote leaves untouched — the defense against "sound without sense" decay. Lines only, and only lines that have a translation; appended to the graduated rotation the same way shadowing gates on reference audio. Manual sessions filter to translated lines.
