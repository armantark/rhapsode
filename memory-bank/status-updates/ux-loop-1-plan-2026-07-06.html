UX LOOP · ITERATION 1 · PLAN

Passage deletion, living library cards, dialog focus

First iteration of the continuous-UX goal: one structural gap (you literally cannot delete a passage), one dead surface (library cards say nothing about your progress), one a11y nit.

Structural gap

Delete a passage — safely

No DELETE /passages/{id} exists. The DB cascades are already sound (revisions → segments → annotations/review states/notes; sessions go with their passage), but media files would orphan on disk — the endpoint unlinks each revision's assets first. UI: a danger-zone row on the passage page with an explicit confirm naming what dies (practice history included).

router.pypassages/[id]/+page.svelteclient.ts

Library cards that know your progress

A11y nit

Focus the shortcuts dialog on open

The dialog has tabindex="-1" but never receives focus, so screen readers and Esc-from-keyboard land wrong. One $effect to focus it when it mounts.
